About Us

Three passionate photographers from three different countries united to show you amazing places and help you create your own great environment decorated with their photos.
We at DEC Photography love three things most of all:
1. Traveling     2. Take photos of incredible places     3. Share their story with everybody
In our web store we give chance to others to enjoy the views that our lenses capture on our journeys. We make photos that represent variety of photography styles such as Landscape, Urban, Wildlife, Car photography and others. We believe that decoration is key part of bringing comfort in our surroundings and that is why we formed DEC Photography.
